  1501→/// Learning Engine — records when a human employee overrides the matcher's
  1502→/// chosen candidate (e.g. matcher picked candidates[0] but employee confirmed
  1503→/// candidates[1]). These overrides feed back into the `historicalMatch` evidence
  1504→/// signal so the matcher LEARNS from human corrections over time.
  1505→///
  1506→/// Lifecycle:
  1507→///   1. matcher returns candidates[0] as winner (productId=A)
  1508→///   2. employee reviews, decides productId=B (candidates[1]) is correct
  1509→///   3. POST /api/products/match-override records:
  1510→///        inputText, inputNormalized, fromProductId=A, toProductId=B,
  1511→///        chosenAlias, auditId (link to original ProductMatchAudit decision)

  1513→///      override and boosts confidence for productId=B's aliases.

  1515→/// `inputNormalized` is the Arabic-normalized form (via normalizeArabic) so that
  1516→/// minor spelling variations still hit the same override record. The compound
