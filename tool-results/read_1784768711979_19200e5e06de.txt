  1594→  1594→/// Learning Engine — records when a human employee overrides the matcher's
  1595→  1595→/// chosen candidate (e.g. matcher picked candidates[0] but employee confirmed
  1596→  1596→/// candidates[1]). These overrides feed back into the `historicalMatch` evidence
  1597→  1597→/// signal so the matcher LEARNS from human corrections over time.
  1598→  1598→///
  1599→  1599→/// Lifecycle:
  1600→  1600→///   1. matcher returns candidates[0] as winner (productId=A)
  1601→  1601→///   2. employee reviews, decides productId=B (candidates[1]) is correct
  1602→  1602→///   3. POST /api/products/match-override records:
  1603→  1603→///        inputText, inputNormalized, fromProductId=A, toProductId=B,
  1604→  1604→///        chosenAlias, auditId (link to original ProductMatchAudit decision)

  1606→  1606→///      override and boosts confidence for productId=B's aliases.
